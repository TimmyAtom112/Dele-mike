About the Artist

Dele Mike is an Artist that is passionate about gospel music with a calling to minister through music. With years of experience performing at gospel events, concerts,and shows.

He's also a Juju and live band musician, bringing the rich sounds of traditional and contemporary African music to life. With years of experience performing for diverse audiences, he create vibrant, soulful performances that connect hearts and celebrate culture. Music is his way of telling stories, spreading joy, and creating unforgettable moments through rhythm, melody, and energy.
